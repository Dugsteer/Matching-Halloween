HALLOWEEN MATCH

How to Play

  • Click on two cards to reveal pictures and names of things we see at Halloween.
  • ABCE and 1234 can be used to identify cards when the game is used on a big screen.
    • The cards are shuffled during each game. Click the buttons to change the sets.

Make a random choice from a set of Halloween words such as bat, witch, cat and monster, or secretly enter your own word in this seasonally scary (not really!) game that helps to practise spelling and the pronunciation of letters of the alphabet. Enter the letters one by one onto the big tombstone, but if you get a letter wrong, one of the little tombstones turns into a ghost and flies away. When there are none left — you lose!
